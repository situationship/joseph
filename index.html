About Joseph

Joseph Hanbin Lee (born January 25, 1999) is a Korean-American UX Designer and social media influencer.

Early Life

Joseph Hanbin Lee was born on January 25 1999 in Texas. He grew up in South Korea before moving back to the United States to attend New York University, where he graduated in 2020 with a degree in Integrated Digital Media.

Career

Design Career
While receiving his education at NYU, Joseph explored many creative mediums including graphic design, motion graphics, UX design, and marketing, prospering at all of them. After graduating, he landed a role as a UX designer at a startup in Seoul.

Social Media Career
Joseph began posting videos on TikTok in late 2019. He quickly gained traction after posting dance covers and K-Pop related content. As of January 2022, he has garnered over 75k followers and 4.8 million likes on his platform.

Personal Life

Joseph resides in Seoul, Korea. He is an avid dancer and Netflix documentary watcher. One of his favorite restaurants is The Wei, once located in Downtown Brooklyn, NY. Joseph is an Aquarius sun, Taurus moon, and Libra rising.
